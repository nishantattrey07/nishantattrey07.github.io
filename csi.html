About Us

We at CSI simply strive for uplifting the computer science community, and even more so, we strive to make a positive impact on one another. We keep our core team closely knit and constantly come up with new ideas. Be it campaigns, events, study groups and what not.

Through our chapter, students get boundless opportunities and marvelous exposures beyond the confined textbook syllabi. It has been a successful journey for our chapter till date in the form of workshops, hackathons, learning weekends, competitions and educational visits, all of which with much more, continues to be delivered by us.
